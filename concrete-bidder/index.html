Common Formulas in Concrete Construction Bidding

  • Concrete Volume: Length (ft) x Width (ft) x Thickness (ft) / 27 = Volume (cubic yards)
  • Rebar Weight: (Length (ft) / 3.28) x (Bar Diameter (mm) x Bar Diameter (mm) / 162) = Weight (kg) Note: Convert kg to lbs if needed (1 kg ≈ 2.20462 lbs)
  • Formwork Area: Length (ft) x Height (ft) = Area (square feet)
  • Labor Cost: Labor Hours x Hourly Rate = Labor Cost
  • Equipment Cost: (Rental Cost) + (Owned Equipment Hours x Owned Equipment Rate) + (Fuel Cost) + (Transport Cost) = Total Equipment Cost
  • Total Cost: Concrete Cost + Materials Cost + Labor Cost + Equipment Cost + Other Costs = Total Cost
  • Profit Margin: (Total Revenue - Total Cost) / Total Revenue x 100% = Profit Margin (%)
